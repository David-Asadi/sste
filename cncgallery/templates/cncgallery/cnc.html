Welcome to Supreme CNC

Already an active competitor in the sub-contract machining sector for the past few years and after recently increasing the CNC machining capacity.

  • Supreme CNC is currently supplying some large locally, nationally and internationally based companies with precision machined components.
  • Servicing a variety of industries and using a wide range of exotic and non exotic materials, working closely with customer procurement and quality departments to develop prototypes and establish manufacturing processes to ensure a consistent and economic supply chain solution.
  • As a quality driven supplier, Supreme CNC strives to excel in its pursuit for customer satisfaction and therefore operates a 100% inspection process for all manufacturing processes
  • Coupled with the latest cad cam software and program storage solutions, Piece of mind is guaranteed with a delivery from Supreme CNC.
  • Supreme has now expanded its machining side of the business (Supreme CNC), which has not only attracted some high profile OEM companies but also a new highly skilled work force.
